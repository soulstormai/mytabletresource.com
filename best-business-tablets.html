The 4 Business Tablets That Pass Our Test

#1 — iPad Pro M4 — Best for Apple Ecosystem Teams

Center Stage keeps you perfectly framed in video calls automatically. Face ID is the fastest biometric unlock we've tested on any device. The Magic Keyboard adds a trackpad that makes iPadOS feel genuinely like a laptop OS. And the M4 chip means you will never, ever hit a performance wall in a business workflow.

I run a 12-person agency entirely from my iPad Pro M4. Client calls, proposal writing, spreadsheet review, PDF contracts, email. The only thing I still need my MacBook for is our accounting software. For everything else, iPad wins.

— Agency founder, LinkedIn post, March 2026 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

#2 — Samsung Galaxy Tab S10 Ultra — Best Desktop Mode

Samsung DeX is the killer feature for business users. Connect a monitor and keyboard and it becomes a full desktop environment — taskbar, resizable windows, file management. If you travel with clients and need to set up a temporary workstation, nothing beats it.

#3 — Microsoft Surface Pro 11 — Best for Windows Users

If your company runs Windows-only software, the Surface Pro 11 is the only tablet that doesn't require compromise. Full Windows 11, real USB-A ports, the best keyboard cover in the business, and Intel/Snapdragon processor options. It's a laptop that happens to have a touchscreen — in the best way.

#4 — iPad Air M2 — Best Value for Business

Same chip as last year's iPad Pro, $400 cheaper, handles 95% of the same tasks. If your company won't reimburse the Pro, the Air M2 is the smart move. You will not feel the difference in email or presentations.
