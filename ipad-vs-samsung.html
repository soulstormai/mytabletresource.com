Performance: M4 vs Dimensity 9300+

Apple dropped the M4 chip into the iPad Pro — the same chip that landed in MacBooks. In our tests running DaVinci Resolve and 3D rendering, the iPad Pro didn't break a sweat. Samsung's Dimensity 9300+ handles everyday tasks without hesitation but throttles earlier in sustained workloads.

I switched from the Galaxy Tab S9 Ultra to the iPad Pro M4 for video editing. What took 8 minutes on Samsung took under 3 minutes on the iPad. Sold.

— Verified Amazon purchaser, March 2026 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Display: Two Great Screens, One Embarrassing Detail

Both use OLED and both look stunning. Samsung is bigger (14.6" vs 13") with anti-reflective coating on all models — Apple's nano-texture glass is $1,799+ only. But Apple hits 1,600 nits vs Samsung's 930, and supports Dolby Vision. Samsung only does HDR10+.

The Thing Nobody Mentions The Samsung Galaxy Tab S10 Ultra has a notch. In 2026. On a $1,200 tablet. Let that sink in.

The S Pen Situation

Samsung includes the S Pen in the box. Apple charges $129 extra for the Apple Pencil Pro. If you draw, take notes, or annotate, Samsung just saved you $129 on day one.
