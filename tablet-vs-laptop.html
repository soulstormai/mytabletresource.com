⚡ No-Nonsense Answer

Get a laptop if you write code, use specialized desktop software, or type more than 3 hours a day. Get a tablet if you consume, create visually, present, or move around constantly.

Most people who ask "tablet or laptop?" actually need both. But if you can only have one — here's your answer.
