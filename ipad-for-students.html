The Student Setup That Actually Works

The iPad alone isn't enough for serious note-taking. Here's the full setup most students end up with anyway — might as well plan for it:

Essential Accessories

  • Apple Pencil (Gen 1 for iPad 10th, Pro for Air/Pro)
  • Folio case with keyboard for typing essays
  • Goodnotes or Notability app ($15-18 one time)
  • USB-C hub if you need HDMI for presentations

Don't Waste Money On

  • Apple Magic Keyboard ($299 — use a $50 third party)
  • 256GB if you use cloud storage (64GB is enough)
  • Cellular model (campus wifi is everywhere)
  • AppleCare+ (use credit card purchase protection instead)
